FAQ - Panda Hunt

Is Panda Hunt free to play?

Yes! Panda Hunt is completely free to download. There are no ads and never will be. Optional gem packs are available if you'd like to support development.

Can I play offline?

Absolutely. Panda Hunt is fully offline - you can play anywhere, anytime without an internet connection.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to the Settings or Store screen in the game and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID you used to purchase.

My progress disappeared - what happened?

Progress is saved locally on your device. If you reinstalled the app or switched devices, progress may not carry over. Contact us and we'll do our best to help.

How do power-ups work?

Power-ups are found inside Lucky Gift tiles and earned through the daily spin wheel. Each one has a unique effect - discover what they do as you play!
