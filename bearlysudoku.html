About the game

Sudoku as a journey.

Bearly Sudoku is a nature-themed Sudoku game for iOS that turns a classic puzzle into a relaxing journey through beautiful worlds. Whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned solver, there's a mode made for you.

Play your way
🗺️

Adventure Mode

Progress through hand-crafted levels across multiple worlds, earning stars and companions along the way.

🧘

Zen Mode

Endless calm puzzles at your chosen difficulty. No pressure, no failure - just pure puzzle flow.

📅

Daily Challenge

One new puzzle every day, shared by players worldwide. Come back every day to keep your streak.

Features
  • 🌿 Explore lush nature-themed worlds
  • 🦊 Unique companions with special abilities
  • 🎨 Unlock board skins and cosmetics
  • ⭐ Earn stars and rewards through gameplay
  • 📈 Player profile and quest tracking
  • 🆓 Free to play - optional cosmetic purchases
  • 🌍 Beautiful animated board effects
  • 🤝 Bring allies to help through tricky puzzles
